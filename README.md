# Healthcare Dashboard


## 🎯 The Business Challenge
Healthcare providers are experiencing increasing patient demand and rising revenue, but operational performance is not improving at the same pace. This creates pressure on hospitals, resources, and long-term sustainability.



## Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:

* **Patient demographics (age, gender, blood type)**
* **Hospital admissions across states and facilities**
* **Medical conditions (e.g., hypertension, diabetes, cancer)**
* **Key metrics: total patients, billing, and length of stay**

## 💡 Key Insights Discovered

**Rising Demand but Stagnant Efficiency:**
* Patient volume increased by 7% YoY, with total patients reaching 55,500
* Billing also grew to 1.42bn
However, the average length of stay (15.51 days) showed no improvement
  
  👉 This indicates inefficient patient flow and resource utilization


**Overdependence on Key Hospitals & Regions:**
* A few hospitals (e.g., top-performing facilities) handle disproportionately high patient volumes
* States like Massachusetts and Texas dominate admissions, while others lag

 👉 This creates capacity strain in some areas and underutilization in others

## 💡 Recommendations
**Improve Operational Efficiency:**
 
* Implement patient flow optimization systems
* Use predictive analytics to reduce unnecessary hospital stays
* Strengthen discharge planning processes

**Balance Resource Distribution:**

* Introduce referral and load-balancing systems across hospitals
* Invest in underutilized regions and facilities
* Expand telemedicine and decentralized care options

**Focus on Preventive & Chronic Care:**

* Launch early screening programs for hypertension and related conditions
* Develop chronic disease management plans
* Promote lifestyle and community health initiatives
